name: Never Fabricate Content
description: Critical rule — never invent materials, features, or details for projects or content
type: feedback
---

Never fabricate content details. Don't invent specific materials, techniques, or features for renovation projects.

**Why:** During the first OpenClaw session (2026-03-23), AI enriched 11 project descriptions with made-up materials (quartz countertops, subway tiles, etc.) that didn't match actual projects. User caught it and was very clear: "everything must be truthful."

**How to apply:** Only improve/expand based on EXISTING data. If content is thin, FLAG it for human review rather than filling with fiction. Trust is more important than word count. This applies to all content — website pages, social media posts, ad copy, email responses.
